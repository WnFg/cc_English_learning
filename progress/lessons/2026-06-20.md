# Lesson 1 — 2026-06-20

**Week:** 1 — Self-introduction & small talk
**Goal:** Use articles (a/an/the) correctly in everyday phrases
**Skill mix:** Listening + Speaking

## Materials used
- Listening passage: two colleagues (Sarah & David) meeting for the first time (~95 words, Samantha voice r170)
- Role-play: student introduced themselves to a new colleague "Alex" (4 exchanges)

## Wrap-up

📚 **Expressions learned:**
- I used to work at...
- mainly on [topic]
- Let's grab coffee sometime

❗ **Key errors:**
- "a RD" → "an RD" (vowel sound rule)
- "responsible for develop" → "responsible for developing" (gerund after "responsible for")
- "success project" → "a successful project" (adjective form)
- "improve the ability" → "improve my ability" (possessive)

📝 **Homework:** Write 5 sentences introducing yourself to a new colleague (previous company, role, team responsibility, reason for joining).

🎯 **Next lesson focus:** Past tense storytelling — telling a colleague about a problem you solved at work.
