# Expressions Learned

| expression | scene | example | date |
|------------|-------|---------|------|
| I used to work at... | introducing yourself, talking about past jobs | I used to work at Baidu for three years. | 2026-06-20 |
| mainly on [topic] | describing your role briefly | I work in the product team, mainly on mobile apps. | 2026-06-20 |
| Let's grab coffee sometime | friendly suggestion to meet up | That's great. Let's grab coffee sometime this week! | 2026-06-20 |
| it turned out (that)... | revealing the surprising cause of a problem | It turned out the SSO token didn't match the environment. | 2026-06-21 |
| in the end, I managed to... | ending a story with what you finally achieved | In the end, I managed to fix it before the deployment. | 2026-06-21 |
| you got a minute? | asking a colleague if they're free to talk | Hey, you got a minute? I wanted to tell you something. | 2026-06-21 |
| a bit of a nightmare | describing a difficult/messy situation (口语，夸张语气) | The migration was a bit of a nightmare. | 2026-06-22 |
| went smoothly | things progressed without problems | After that, the deployment went smoothly. | 2026-06-22 |
| at the last minute | just before the deadline, barely in time | We pushed the fix at the last minute. | 2026-06-22 |
| How should I put this… | filler when searching for the right word | How should I put this… the system just stopped working. | 2026-06-22 |
| meanwhile | at the same time, in parallel | Meanwhile, the QA team was waiting. | 2026-06-22 |
| What a relief! | expressing that something stressful is finally resolved | It finally worked. What a relief! | 2026-06-22 |
