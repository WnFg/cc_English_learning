## Wrap-up

### 🧠 今日核心总结 (Key takeaways)

- **词汇 Vocabulary:** deadline (截止日期), eventually (最终), meanwhile (与此同时), relief (如释重负), went smoothly (进展顺利), a bit of a nightmare (有点糟糕)
- **短语 Phrases (incl. filler):** Timeframes: "by Friday / two hours later / at the last minute"; Transitions: "so / after that / meanwhile / in the meantime"; Filler: "How should I put this…" (buy 2 seconds when stuck)
- **语法 Grammar:** Passive voice = **wasn't + past participle** (no extra "be") — ❌ wasn't be affected → ✅ wasn't affected; Time prepositions — **on** Wednesday, no preposition before last/next/this week
- **发音 Pronunciation:** "eventually" = e-**VENT**-chually (4 syllables); "meanwhile" = **MEAN**-while (stress on first)

### ❗ Key errors
- "wasn't be affected / wasn't be relate to" → "wasn't affected / wasn't related to" (passive voice: wasn't + past participle)
- "in Wednesday / in last week" → "on Wednesday / last week" (time prepositions)
- "the process wasn't smoothly" → "didn't go smoothly" (adverb needs a verb: go/run/work out smoothly)
